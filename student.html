从这里开始Where it begins

先认识自己，
再设定你的目标First know yourself,
then set your goal.

你已经了解我们的课程了。真正的第一步，不是急着测程度，而是先认识自己怎么学、想在哪里被看见——目标清楚了，路自然就清楚了。You already know our course. The real first step isn't rushing to test your level — it's understanding how you learn and where you want to be seen. Once the goal is clear, the path becomes clear.

第一步Step 1 性格测试——认识自己Personality test — know yourself

一份 MBTI 式的学习性格测试：你是哪种学习者、什么方式最适合你、压力大时怎么调整。没有对错，只有更懂自己。这也是你学习旅程的真正起点。An MBTI-style learning-personality test: what kind of learner you are, what works best for you, how you respond under pressure. No right or wrong — just knowing yourself better. This is the real start of your journey.

第二步Step 2 设定你的目标——想在哪里被看见Set your goal — where you want to be seen

开会敢发言？见客户不再心虚？还是升职、外派接得住？把你真正想达到的画面说出来，顾问会陪你把它拆成一步步可走的目标。Speaking up in meetings? Facing clients with confidence? Ready for a promotion or posting? Name the picture you truly want — your advisor helps you break it into steps you can actually walk.

第三步 · 免费Step 3 · Free 定位测试——找到起点Placement test — find your start

准备好了，才做定位测试。60 题 · 没有及格线、没有评判，只为找到「刚刚好」的 Level，让你从对的地方出发——不会太难跟不上，也不会太简单浪费时间。When you're ready, take the placement test. 60 questions · no pass mark, no judgement — just to find the level that's right, so you start where it fits: never too hard to follow, never too easy to waste time.

The grant is released in batches, and each batch lasts only while the funds do. The price you see today reflects this batch’s grant.
